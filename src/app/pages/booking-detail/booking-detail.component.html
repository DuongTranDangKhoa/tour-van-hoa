Intro Description

✨ Step into the heart of Central Vietnam with the Vietnam Art & Culture Tour by This Is Home — a full-day cultural journey designed to connect you with the country’s authentic heritage. From hands-on craft workshops to exploring the Cham Towers, every moment reveals the timeless beauty of Vietnam’s traditions. Guided by passionate locals, this immersive adventure blends creativity, cuisine, and community, leaving you with unforgettable memories of Vietnam’s soul.

Highlights

🌿 Authentic cultural immersion with local artisans.

🏺 Pottery-making at Lê Đức Hạ Pottery Village, including your own handmade souvenir.

☕ Hands-on coffee brewing workshop at Hush Coffee Workspace.

🪵 Wood carving experience at Âu Lạc Workshop with master artisans.

🍲 Savor authentic Vietnamese cuisine with a traditional lunch.

🏛 Explore the Cham Towers, learning about the Champa Kingdom’s art, history, and architecture.

🎁 Take home a cultural keepsake from your craft experience.

Full Description

🌸 This Is Home brings you a cultural journey to remember through Central Vietnam. Guided by local artisans and cultural experts, you'll experience:

  • Pottery-making at Lê Đức Hạ Village, where Cham traditions meet Vietnamese craft.
  • A hands-on coffee workshop at Hush Workspace, discovering the cultural roots of Vietnam's coffee heritage.
  • Wood carving at Âu Lạc Workshop, learning how traditional art reflects history and spirituality.
  • A visit to the Cham Towers in Đà Nẵng, where the architecture, art, and stories of the Champa Kingdom come alive.

    • Along the way, enjoy a traditional Vietnamese lunch and take home a meaningful keepsake from your own craftwork.

    🌱 Whether you're a culture enthusiast, a family seeking meaningful bonding, or a traveler craving authentic encounters — this tour promises lasting memories of Vietnam's heritage and soul.
